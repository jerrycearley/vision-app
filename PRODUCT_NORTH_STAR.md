# Product North Star (JC)

## Why this matters
As humanity enters an age of abundance—AI/robots doing most work and traditional jobs vanishing—people will need ways to find:
- meaning
- purpose
- satisfaction
- compensation

## What the app should do
Help users earn tokens for achievements such as:
- completing goals
- educating themselves
- gaining real skills
- making progress on roadmaps/milestones

## Guiding belief
As AI becomes more advanced, the best chance for humanity to live in harmony is increasing people’s:
- wisdom
- deeper understanding of the world
- deeper understanding of themselves

## Product principle
Make every effort to design and implement features that move users toward meaningful growth, wisdom, and self-understanding—while providing tangible rewards (tokens) for real progress.
